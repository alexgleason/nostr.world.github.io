FAQ

What's nostrasia?

A free freedom conference, bringing together nostriches from different corners of the globe for conversations on everything nostr.

What's the cost of entry to Nostrasia?

Tickets are free

In-person tickets are limited. Please make sure to register.

When & where's Nostrasia?

Nov 1-3, 2023

Tokyo & Hong Kong

What's nostr?

It is a different kind of social media. You keep your follows, followers, and notes across any number of nostr products, apps & websites.

Who's Nostr's CEO?

Trick question. No one.
Nostr is an open protocol, which means any developer can use it to build their own products, yet we can all connect to the same nostr network regardless of which product, app or website we use.

How do I start on nostr?

You can start the journey by downloading a nostr app on your phone. Recommended for beginners:
Android: Amethyst.
Apple: Damus.

Tokyo's Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Book excursions and/or day passes ahead of time instead of same-day.
  • Drive, ride, walk on the left side of the street.
  • Limit public displays of affection that involve kissing.
  • Make sure that you have consent if/when taking a photograph that has another person in it.
  • If you want to travel around Japan before/after the conference, you may want to consider Japan Rail Pass for packaged JR tickets

Don'ts

  • Speak loudly on bullet or metro trains. Keep conversation to a minimum and quiet if/when communicating anything.
  • Wear shoes indoors; this includes locations wherein meals are served on traditional Tatami Mats.
  • Eat on-the-go. If eating on-the-go is necessary for your health, use discretion.
  • Leave gratuity when dining
